Davenport City

Pounce the Panther is the Mascot of Davenport University

  1. Pounce is usually represented as a stylized panther with blue fur, sharp white teeth with protruding canines, and black whiskers.
  2. He usually wears either the men's basketball outfit or the University football uniform
  3. Pounce is 6'0 with a 3 foot tail and favors the colors royal blue and white.
  4. He communicates via hand gestures and sign language, rather than speech.
